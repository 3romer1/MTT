Communities We Serve

Orange County spans a diverse business landscape, from coastal innovation hubs to established industrial centers. We work with organizations across the region, with particular depth in the following communities.

Irvine

Home to our Orange County office. Irvine's master-planned business districts along the Spectrum and Great Park neighborhoods host a dense concentration of technology, biotech, and professional services firms that rely on sophisticated IT infrastructure.

Newport Beach

A center for financial services, wealth management, and private equity firms. These organizations require rigorous data security, regulatory compliance, and the kind of white-glove IT support that matches their client experience standards.

Costa Mesa

A growing hub for creative agencies, retail headquarters, and mid-market enterprises. Costa Mesa businesses often need scalable cloud solutions and modern collaboration platforms to support distributed teams.

Anaheim

Orange County's largest city by population, with a strong base of hospitality, manufacturing, and logistics companies. These industries demand high-availability systems, IoT readiness, and resilient network architectures.

Huntington Beach

An expanding commercial market with healthcare providers, energy companies, and professional services firms. Huntington Beach organizations benefit from proactive managed IT that reduces downtime and strengthens compliance.

Mission Viejo

South Orange County's business corridor serves healthcare systems, education technology companies, and growing professional firms that need enterprise-grade IT without building large internal teams.
