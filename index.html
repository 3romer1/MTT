Frequently Asked Questions

What IT leaders ask before partnering with MTT.

What is a fractional CIO and how can it help my business?

A fractional or virtual CIO provides senior-level IT leadership on a part-time or advisory basis. MTT Consulting's vCIO service gives you a strategic IT partner who owns your technology roadmap, aligns IT spending to business goals, manages vendors, and reports to leadership — without the $200K-$400K cost of a full-time executive hire. Engagement models range from 4 hours per month advisory to 32 hours per month full fractional leadership.

Does MTT provide on-site IT services in Collin County and Orange County?

Yes. MTT Consulting is headquartered in Frisco, Texas and provides on-site IT consulting, managed services, and infrastructure support throughout Collin County — including Frisco, McKinney, Plano, Allen, and Prosper. We also have a physical presence in Irvine, California, serving businesses across Orange County including Newport Beach and Costa Mesa. Remote services are available nationwide.

What cybersecurity services does MTT Consulting offer?

We provide comprehensive cybersecurity services including security assessments, penetration testing, firewall management, endpoint detection and response (EDR/XDR), identity and access management, compliance alignment (HIPAA, SOC 2, PCI DSS, CMMC), virtual CISO services, incident response planning, and 24/7 managed security operations.

Does MTT help with cloud migration and Azure consulting?

Yes. We provide multi-cloud strategy across Azure, AWS, and Google Cloud — including cloud migration, Microsoft 365 optimization, hybrid cloud architecture, FinOps cost management, and cloud security. Most clients see a 25-40% reduction in cloud costs within the first six months of engagement.

What is MTT Consulting's approach to AI governance?

We take a govern-first, deploy-second approach to AI adoption. We help organizations build AI governance frameworks including acceptable use policies, data handling guardrails, tool evaluation criteria, compliance alignment (EU AI Act, NIST AI RMF), and executive readiness programs — before deploying AI tools into production environments.

How does MTT help organizations adopt AI?

Our AI Enablement practice covers the full adoption lifecycle: an AI readiness assessment of your data, security, and infrastructure; governance frameworks and acceptable-use policies aligned to NIST AI RMF and the EU AI Act; secure deployment of tools like Microsoft 365 Copilot with identity and data-loss prevention configured first; and role-based workforce training with ROI reporting — so AI adoption is governed, secure, and measurable from day one.

What industries does MTT Consulting serve?

We serve healthcare organizations, financial services firms, manufacturing companies, technology startups, professional services firms, and government agencies. The common thread is mid-size organizations (50-500 employees) that need enterprise-grade IT strategy, cybersecurity, and infrastructure support without the overhead of a large consulting firm.

How does MTT's procurement service work?

MTT acts as a single procurement partner for all IT purchases — hardware, software, licensing, and cloud subscriptions. We leverage vendor relationships with Cisco, Fortinet, Dell, Microsoft, Palo Alto Networks, and others to provide volume pricing typically reserved for larger organizations. We handle the entire lifecycle from requirements analysis to warranty management.

How is MTT different from a typical managed services provider?

Most MSPs focus on reactive ticket resolution and basic monitoring. MTT Consulting starts with business strategy — understanding your revenue model, growth targets, and compliance obligations before recommending any technology. We embed senior-level engineers and advisory into your organization, provide vendor-neutral guidance, and measure success by business outcomes rather than tickets closed.
