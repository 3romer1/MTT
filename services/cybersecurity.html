Cybersecurity & Resilience

Protecting your organization from modern cyber threats with layered defenses, proactive monitoring, and resilience planning that keeps your business running when others go dark.

Cybersecurity is no longer an IT problem. It is a business survival problem. Mid-market organizations face the same threats as enterprises but often lack the dedicated security teams and budgets to match. MTT Consulting bridges that gap with practical, right-sized cybersecurity services that protect what matters most without creating operational friction.

The Evolving Threat Landscape

The threat environment has shifted dramatically. Ransomware attacks now target organizations of every size, with mid-market companies increasingly in the crosshairs precisely because attackers know they are less likely to have mature defenses in place. Phishing campaigns have become highly sophisticated, leveraging AI-generated content that bypasses traditional email filters. Supply chain attacks compromise trusted vendors and propagate laterally into your environment before anyone realizes something is wrong.

Mid-market organizations are targeted disproportionately for several reasons: they hold valuable data, they often run legacy systems with known vulnerabilities, and they frequently lack the security operations capacity to detect threats in real time. The average cost of a breach continues to climb, and for organizations without cyber insurance or incident response plans, a single event can threaten business continuity.

The question is no longer whether your organization will face a cyber incident. It is whether you will be prepared when it happens.

Our Security Services

MTT Consulting delivers a comprehensive portfolio of cybersecurity services designed for organizations that need enterprise-grade protection without enterprise-level complexity.

Identity & Access Management

Identity is the new perimeter. We implement and manage conditional access policies, multi-factor authentication, privileged access management, and single sign-on solutions that ensure the right people have access to the right resources at the right time. We harden your identity infrastructure to prevent credential-based attacks, which remain the leading initial access vector.

Endpoint Detection & Response (EDR/XDR)

Traditional antivirus is no longer sufficient. Our endpoint detection and response services deploy advanced behavioral analysis and threat intelligence across every device in your environment. Extended detection and response extends this visibility across email, cloud workloads, and network telemetry to correlate threats that span multiple attack surfaces.

Email Security & Phishing Defense

Email remains the primary attack vector for most breaches. We deploy advanced email filtering, link and attachment sandboxing, impersonation protection, and user awareness training to create a layered defense against phishing, business email compromise, and social engineering attacks.

Backup Validation & Disaster Recovery

Backups only matter if they work when you need them. We design and validate backup architectures with immutable storage, air-gapped copies, and automated recovery testing. Our disaster recovery planning ensures your critical systems can be restored within defined recovery time and recovery point objectives.

Security Assessments & Penetration Testing

You cannot protect what you do not understand. Our security assessments provide a clear picture of your current posture, including vulnerability scanning, configuration reviews, and targeted penetration testing that simulates real-world attack techniques against your environment.

Compliance Alignment

Regulatory requirements are not optional, and the penalties for non-compliance continue to increase. We help organizations align their security controls with the frameworks that matter to their industry, including HIPAA, SOC 2, PCI DSS, and CMMC. Our approach treats compliance as a byproduct of good security, not a checkbox exercise.

The MTT Security Assessment

Every engagement begins with understanding where you stand today. Our security assessment follows a structured three-phase approach that delivers actionable results, not a binder that collects dust on a shelf.

Phase 1 — Discovery

Asset Inventory & Vulnerability Scanning

We catalog your entire technology environment: endpoints, servers, cloud resources, SaaS applications, network devices, and shadow IT. Automated vulnerability scanning identifies known weaknesses across your infrastructure, while manual review catches configuration gaps that scanners miss. The result is a complete picture of your attack surface.

Phase 2 — Analysis

Risk Scoring & Gap Identification

Raw vulnerability data without context is noise. We score each finding based on exploitability, business impact, and compensating controls. Gaps are mapped against your industry's regulatory requirements and best-practice frameworks. This phase transforms technical findings into business risk language that leadership can act on.

Phase 3 — Remediation

Prioritized Roadmap & Implementation

We deliver a prioritized remediation roadmap organized by risk severity and implementation effort. Quick wins are addressed immediately while longer-term initiatives are scheduled into a realistic timeline. Our team can execute remediation directly or work alongside your internal IT staff to close gaps systematically.

Incident Response Planning

Every organization needs an incident response plan before an incident occurs. The time to figure out who does what, who communicates with whom, and how systems are isolated is not during an active breach. It is months before one ever happens.

MTT Consulting helps organizations build and maintain incident response capabilities that include:

  • Incident response plans with clearly defined roles, escalation paths, and decision-making authority for different incident severity levels
  • Tabletop exercises that walk your leadership and technical teams through realistic breach scenarios to test decision-making under pressure
  • Technical runbooks with step-by-step procedures for containing and eradicating common threat types including ransomware, business email compromise, and data exfiltration
  • Communication protocols covering internal notification chains, customer and partner communications, regulatory reporting obligations, and media response procedures

Organizations that practice their incident response regularly recover faster, suffer less data loss, and maintain stakeholder trust through transparent, coordinated action.

Managed Security vs. Project-Based

We recognize that every organization has different needs and internal capabilities. That is why we offer two engagement models designed to meet you where you are.

Managed Security Services

For organizations that need ongoing, continuous security operations, our managed security model provides persistent monitoring, threat detection, vulnerability management, and incident response on a predictable monthly basis. We function as an extension of your team, providing the depth of expertise and around-the-clock vigilance that would otherwise require a fully staffed internal security operations center. This model is ideal for organizations without a dedicated CISO or security team.

Project-Based Engagements

For organizations with specific security objectives or existing internal capabilities, our project-based model delivers targeted outcomes within a defined scope and timeline. This includes security assessments, penetration tests, compliance readiness audits, incident response plan development, and architecture reviews. Project-based work can also serve as a starting point to evaluate whether a managed relationship is the right long-term fit.

The strongest security programs combine proactive planning with the operational capacity to detect and respond to threats in real time. Whether managed or project-based, MTT Consulting ensures your organization is prepared for what comes next.
