Modern Networks for Distributed Organizations

The way businesses operate has fundamentally changed. Remote and hybrid workforces, multi-site operations, and cloud-first application strategies have made traditional hub-and-spoke network architectures a liability rather than a foundation. Legacy MPLS circuits that once served as the backbone of enterprise connectivity now struggle to keep pace with the bandwidth demands of SaaS platforms, real-time collaboration tools, and cloud-hosted workloads.

MTT Consulting designs network architectures that reflect how modern organizations actually work. Whether you are connecting five branch offices or fifty, supporting a fully remote workforce, or migrating critical applications to the cloud, our network engineering team builds infrastructure that delivers consistent performance, security, and reliability across every location and every user.

We take a holistic view of your connectivity needs, from the physical layer through application delivery, ensuring that every component of your network works together as a coherent system rather than a collection of point solutions.

SD-WAN Design & Deployment

Software-defined wide area networking has become the standard for organizations that need agile, cost-effective multi-site connectivity. But the difference between an SD-WAN deployment that transforms your network and one that becomes an expensive headache comes down to architecture, not appliances. MTT Consulting takes a vendor-agnostic approach to SD-WAN, recommending and deploying the platform that best fits your specific requirements rather than pushing a single vendor's solution.

Our SD-WAN expertise spans the leading platforms:

  • Cisco Viptela and Meraki: Enterprise-grade orchestration with deep integration into existing Cisco environments, ideal for organizations with established Cisco infrastructure
  • Fortinet Secure SD-WAN: Converged networking and security with industry-leading next-generation firewall capabilities built directly into the SD-WAN fabric
  • Palo Alto Prisma SD-WAN: Cloud-native architecture with autonomous path selection and tight integration with Prisma Access for comprehensive SASE deployments

Every deployment begins with a thorough analysis of your current circuit inventory, application traffic patterns, and performance requirements. We design application-aware routing policies that steer business-critical traffic over the optimal path in real time, ensuring that voice, video, and mission-critical applications always get the performance they need.

For many organizations, SD-WAN delivers 40 to 60 percent cost savings over traditional MPLS by leveraging broadband, LTE, and 5G circuits alongside or in place of dedicated private links. We help you right-size your circuit portfolio to maximize performance while minimizing monthly recurring costs.

Enterprise Wireless Design

Wireless connectivity is no longer a convenience feature. It is the primary network access method for most users and an increasing number of IoT devices, building systems, and operational technology. A poorly designed wireless network creates productivity bottlenecks, helpdesk tickets, and security vulnerabilities that ripple across the entire organization.

MTT Consulting designs enterprise wireless networks using predictive site surveys and RF modeling tools to ensure complete coverage, appropriate capacity, and minimal interference before a single access point is mounted. For existing deployments, we conduct on-site validation surveys to identify dead zones, co-channel interference, and capacity constraints that are impacting user experience.

Our wireless design capabilities include:

  • High-density design: Conference rooms, auditoriums, trading floors, and open-plan offices require specialized antenna placement, channel planning, and client load balancing to deliver reliable performance under heavy concurrent usage
  • Platform expertise: Deep experience with Ruckus, Aruba, and Ubiquiti platforms allows us to recommend and deploy the right solution for your environment, budget, and management requirements
  • Guest and BYOD segmentation: Secure network segmentation that isolates guest devices, personal BYOD devices, and IoT endpoints from your corporate network while maintaining a seamless user experience
  • Wi-Fi 6E and Wi-Fi 7 readiness: Future-proof designs that take advantage of the 6 GHz spectrum for ultra-high throughput and low latency in environments that demand it

Firewall & Security Architecture

Network security is not a product you install at the perimeter and forget. It is an architecture you design into every layer of your network. MTT Consulting builds security frameworks that protect your organization from both external threats and internal lateral movement, using defense-in-depth principles and modern Zero Trust methodologies.

Our security architecture services cover:

  • Next-generation firewall design: Purpose-built firewall architectures with application-layer inspection, intrusion prevention, SSL/TLS decryption, and advanced threat protection. We design for high availability, throughput requirements, and management simplicity
  • Microsegmentation: Granular network segmentation policies that restrict lateral movement between workloads, users, and device classes. We implement microsegmentation at both the network layer and within SD-WAN and cloud environments
  • Network access control (NAC): Automated device profiling, posture assessment, and policy-based network admission. NAC ensures that only authorized, compliant devices gain access to your network resources
  • Zero Trust network architecture: Identity-driven access policies that verify every user and device before granting access to any resource, regardless of network location. We design Zero Trust frameworks that integrate with your existing identity providers and access management systems

Security cannot be bolted on after the network is built. Every architectural decision, from VLAN design to routing policy to SD-WAN segmentation, has security implications that must be considered from day one.

Multisite Connectivity

Connecting branch offices, regional hubs, data centers, and cloud environments into a cohesive network requires more than just provisioning circuits. It demands a thoughtful architecture that balances performance, cost, redundancy, and operational simplicity. MTT Consulting designs multisite connectivity solutions that grow with your organization and adapt to changing business requirements.

Our multisite design approach addresses:

  • Branch office design: Standardized, repeatable branch network templates that ensure consistent performance, security, and manageability across every location. Each branch design includes local switching, wireless, firewall, and WAN connectivity
  • Hub-and-spoke vs. mesh topologies: We evaluate and recommend the optimal topology for your traffic patterns, application architecture, and business requirements. Many organizations benefit from a hybrid approach that combines centralized hub traffic inspection with direct mesh connectivity for latency-sensitive applications
  • Circuit redundancy: Diverse carrier and transport technology strategies that protect against single points of failure. We design redundancy into every layer, from dual ISP connections to LTE and 5G failover, ensuring business continuity when circuits go down
  • Failover strategies: Automated failover mechanisms with defined recovery time objectives for every site and every application tier. We test failover scenarios during deployment and document procedures for your operations team

Network Monitoring & Optimization

A well-designed network is only as good as your ability to observe, measure, and continuously improve its performance. MTT Consulting implements comprehensive monitoring and optimization programs that give your team complete visibility into network health and the data needed to make informed capacity and architecture decisions.

Our monitoring and optimization services include:

  • Performance baselines: We establish detailed performance baselines for every circuit, site, and application, providing a reference point for identifying degradation before it impacts users. Baselines are updated quarterly to reflect changing usage patterns
  • Traffic analysis: Deep packet inspection and flow analysis that reveals exactly how your bandwidth is being consumed, by which applications, which users, and at which times. Traffic analysis identifies optimization opportunities and informs capacity planning decisions
  • Capacity planning: Data-driven forecasting that projects bandwidth, device, and infrastructure requirements 12 to 24 months into the future. Capacity plans prevent reactive emergency upgrades and enable proactive budget planning
  • Proactive alerting: Intelligent alerting frameworks that notify your team of performance anomalies, threshold violations, and potential failures before they cause outages. We tune alerting thresholds to minimize noise while catching genuine issues early

Ongoing optimization is not optional. Network traffic patterns evolve as your organization adopts new applications, onboards new locations, and shifts workloads between on-premises and cloud environments. Our monitoring programs ensure your network continues to meet performance expectations as your business grows.
