The Network Is the Business

Today's enterprise network carries more weight than ever. Video conferencing, cloud applications, VoIP, IoT devices, and SaaS platforms have turned bandwidth into oxygen. Traditional hub-and-spoke MPLS architectures weren't designed for cloud-first workloads — they route traffic through data centers that no longer host the applications employees need. The result: latency, bottlenecks, user frustration, and IT teams trapped in a cycle of reactive troubleshooting.

MTT Connect was built to solve this. We design, deploy, and manage enterprise networks that are application-aware, resilient, secure, and built to scale. Whether your organization spans five locations or five hundred, MTT Connect delivers a unified networking strategy that aligns infrastructure with how your business actually operates.

SD-WAN: Intelligent Connectivity

Software-defined wide area networking is the foundation of MTT Connect. We take a vendor-agnostic approach, designing and deploying the SD-WAN platform that best fits your environment rather than defaulting to a single vendor's ecosystem. Our engineering team holds deep expertise across the industry's leading platforms.

Platform expertise includes:

  • Cisco Viptela & Meraki: Enterprise-grade orchestration with deep Cisco ecosystem integration, ideal for organizations with established Cisco infrastructure and campus environments
  • Fortinet Secure SD-WAN: Converged networking and security with industry-leading next-generation firewall capabilities built directly into the SD-WAN fabric
  • VMware VeloCloud: Cloud-first SD-WAN architecture with automated multi-path optimization and strong integration with VMware SASE and cloud workloads
  • Palo Alto Prisma SD-WAN: Autonomous path selection with tight Prisma Access integration for comprehensive SASE deployments and cloud-native security

Every deployment begins with analysis of your current circuit inventory, application traffic patterns, and performance requirements. We design application-aware routing policies that steer business-critical traffic — voice, video, ERP, and collaboration tools — over the optimal path in real time using dynamic path selection across broadband, LTE, and MPLS circuits.

Centralized orchestration and policy management gives your team single-pane-of-glass visibility into the entire WAN fabric. Policy changes propagate to every site in minutes rather than weeks, and traffic engineering decisions are driven by real-time telemetry rather than static configurations.

SD-WAN transforms network economics. Organizations that move from pure MPLS to an intelligently designed SD-WAN fabric typically see 40 to 60 percent cost reduction in WAN spend while gaining performance, agility, and resilience that MPLS alone could never deliver.

Enterprise Wireless Architecture

Wireless is the primary access layer for the modern workforce. A poorly designed wireless network creates productivity bottlenecks, helpdesk tickets, and security gaps that ripple across the entire organization. MTT Connect treats wireless as critical infrastructure, not an afterthought.

Our wireless architecture capabilities include:

  • Predictive RF modeling and site surveys: We use advanced RF simulation tools to model coverage, capacity, and interference before a single access point is mounted. For existing environments, on-site validation surveys identify dead zones and co-channel interference impacting performance
  • High-density designs: Purpose-built wireless architectures for warehouses, open-plan offices, healthcare facilities, and hospitality environments that demand reliable connectivity under heavy concurrent device loads
  • Platform expertise: Deep engineering experience across Ruckus, Aruba, Cisco Meraki, and Ubiquiti platforms allows us to recommend and deploy the right solution for your environment, scale, and management requirements
  • Seamless roaming and load balancing: Intelligent client steering, band steering, and AP load balancing ensure consistent performance as users and devices move throughout the facility
  • Guest and BYOD segmentation: Secure network segmentation with NAC integration that isolates guest devices, personal BYOD endpoints, and IoT devices from your corporate network while maintaining a seamless user experience
  • Outdoor and industrial wireless: Ruggedized wireless solutions for campus environments, distribution yards, manufacturing floors, and outdoor facilities where enterprise-grade coverage extends beyond the four walls

Multisite Network Design

Connecting branch offices, regional hubs, data centers, and cloud environments into a cohesive network requires thoughtful architecture that balances performance, cost, redundancy, and operational simplicity. MTT Connect delivers multisite designs that scale from five locations to five hundred without architectural overhaul.

Our multisite design approach covers:

  • Hub-and-spoke, partial mesh, and full mesh topologies: We evaluate traffic patterns, application dependencies, and business requirements to recommend the optimal topology — or a hybrid approach that combines centralized inspection with direct site-to-site connectivity for latency-sensitive applications
  • Dual-ISP redundancy and automatic failover: Diverse carrier and transport technology strategies protect against single points of failure. Every site is designed with defined recovery time objectives and automated failover that keeps the business running when circuits go down
  • DMVPN and IPSec tunnel architectures: Scalable overlay networks that enable secure site-to-site communication across any transport, with dynamic tunnel creation that eliminates the need for static point-to-point configurations at every location
  • QoS policies for voice, video, and data prioritization: End-to-end quality of service frameworks that guarantee performance for real-time applications across every link in the network, from the branch edge to the data center and cloud
  • Centralized management for 5 to 500+ locations: Single-pane orchestration platforms that give your operations team visibility and control across the entire network without requiring site-by-site configuration
  • Zero-touch provisioning for rapid branch deployments: Standardized, repeatable branch templates that enable new site deployments in days rather than weeks. Ship hardware to the site, plug it in, and the configuration pulls automatically from the central controller

Network Security — Built In, Not Bolted On

Security is not a layer you add after the network is deployed. It is an architectural principle woven into every design decision from day one. MTT Connect embeds security into the network fabric using defense-in-depth strategies and modern Zero Trust methodologies.

Our integrated security architecture includes:

  • Next-generation firewall integration: Purpose-built firewall architectures using Fortinet, Palo Alto, and Cisco platforms with application-layer inspection, intrusion prevention, SSL/TLS decryption, and advanced threat protection designed for high availability and throughput
  • Microsegmentation and east-west traffic inspection: Granular segmentation policies that restrict lateral movement between workloads, users, and device classes. We implement microsegmentation at the network layer, within SD-WAN environments, and across cloud workloads
  • NAC (Network Access Control) for device compliance: Automated device profiling, posture assessment, and policy-based network admission that ensures only authorized, compliant devices gain access to network resources
  • Zero Trust network architecture principles: Identity-driven access policies that verify every user and device before granting access to any resource, regardless of network location. Integrated with your existing identity providers and access management systems
  • Encrypted site-to-site and remote access VPN: Secure tunnel architectures for site interconnection and remote workforce access, with split-tunnel and full-tunnel policies aligned to your security posture and compliance requirements
  • DNS-layer security and content filtering: Cloud-delivered DNS protection that blocks malicious domains, phishing attempts, and policy-violating content before connections are ever established

Every architectural decision — from VLAN design to routing policy to SD-WAN segmentation — has security implications. MTT Connect ensures those implications are addressed by design, not discovered after deployment.

Monitoring, Optimization & NOC Services

A well-designed network is only as reliable as your ability to observe, measure, and continuously improve its performance. MTT Connect includes comprehensive monitoring and management services delivered through our 24/7 network operations center.

Our NOC and optimization services include:

  • SNMP, NetFlow, and syslog collection and analysis: Comprehensive telemetry collection that provides deep visibility into device health, traffic flows, and system events across every node in the network
  • Automated alerting and escalation workflows: Intelligent alerting frameworks tuned to minimize noise while catching genuine issues early. Defined escalation paths ensure the right engineer responds to the right issue at the right time
  • Quarterly performance reviews and capacity planning: Data-driven reviews that analyze performance trends, identify optimization opportunities, and forecast bandwidth and infrastructure requirements 12 to 24 months into the future
  • SLA-backed uptime guarantees: Contractual uptime commitments backed by real-time monitoring, proactive maintenance, and defined remediation timelines that hold our team accountable for network reliability
  • Proactive firmware and security patch management: Scheduled maintenance windows for firmware upgrades, security patches, and configuration updates that keep your network current without disrupting business operations

Network traffic patterns evolve constantly as your organization adopts new applications, onboards new locations, and shifts workloads between on-premises and cloud environments. Our monitoring programs ensure your network continues to meet performance expectations as your business grows and changes.
