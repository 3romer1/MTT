Comprehensive cybersecurity operations that protect your organization from modern threats while enabling the business to move faster, not slower.

The cybersecurity landscape has shifted fundamentally. Ransomware attacks have become industrialized. Phishing campaigns use AI-generated content that bypasses traditional filters. Supply chain compromises turn trusted software into attack vectors. Security Is Not a Product — It’s an Outcome

Most mid-market organizations are one well-crafted email away from a business-altering event. The tools exist to prevent it, but tools alone are not enough. Security is not a firewall you install or a checkbox you mark during an audit. It is an outcome that requires continuous effort across people, process, and technology.

MTT Security exists to change that equation. We build, manage, and continuously improve multi-layered security programs that treat cybersecurity as an ongoing discipline — not a one-time project. Every control we deploy, every policy we write, and every alert we investigate serves a single purpose: keeping your organization resilient against threats that evolve every day.

Firewall & Perimeter Defense

Your network perimeter remains the first line of defense, and it demands more than default configurations and annual reviews. MTT Security designs, deploys, and manages next-generation firewall infrastructure that inspects traffic at every layer of the stack.

  • Next-generation firewall design and management across Fortinet FortiGate, Palo Alto PA-Series, and Cisco Firepower platforms
  • Unified threat management (UTM) consolidating firewall, antivirus, content filtering, and intrusion prevention into a single policy framework
  • IDS/IPS configuration and tuning to detect and block malicious traffic patterns without drowning your team in false positives
  • Application-layer inspection and sandboxing to identify threats hiding inside legitimate application protocols
  • SSL/TLS inspection for encrypted traffic that would otherwise pass through uninspected
  • Policy optimization and rule-base cleanup to eliminate redundant, shadowed, and overly permissive rules that accumulate over time

A properly managed perimeter does not just block known threats. It creates visibility into what is entering and leaving your network, giving your security team the data they need to detect anomalies before they become incidents.

Threat Detection & Response

  • EDR/XDR deployment and management across CrowdStrike, SentinelOne, and Microsoft Defender for Endpoint — covering every endpoint, server, and cloud workload in your environment
  • SIEM integration and log correlation to aggregate security telemetry from firewalls, endpoints, identity systems, and cloud platforms into a single pane of glass
  • 24/7 SOC monitoring with a 15-minute SLA for critical alerts — because threats do not wait for business hours
  • Threat intelligence feeds and IOC tracking that continuously update your defenses with indicators of compromise from global threat research
  • Automated response playbooks that isolate compromised endpoints, block malicious IPs, and disable compromised accounts in seconds, not hours
  • Threat hunting and proactive investigation conducted by experienced analysts who search for threats that automated tools miss

Detection without response is just observation. Response without detection is just guessing. MTT Security delivers both in a unified, managed operation.

Identity & Access Management

Credentials are the single most exploited attack vector in modern breaches. Stolen passwords, over-provisioned service accounts, and stale access rights give attackers the keys to move laterally through your environment undetected. Identity and access management is no longer a convenience feature — it is a critical security control.

  • Azure AD / Entra ID hardening with security baselines, legacy authentication blocking, and tenant-level protections
  • Conditional access policies and MFA enforcement that adapt authentication requirements based on user risk, device compliance, and network location
  • Privileged access management (PAM) to vault, rotate, and audit administrative credentials across on-premises and cloud systems
  • SSO integration across SaaS applications to reduce password sprawl and centralize access control
  • Identity governance and lifecycle management ensuring access is provisioned at hire, adjusted at role change, and revoked at departure
  • Zero-standing-privileges and just-in-time access that grant elevated rights only when needed and revoke them automatically when the task is complete

Identity is the new perimeter. In a world where users work from anywhere, on any device, through any network, the only consistent control point is the identity itself. Protect the identity, and you protect the organization.

Incident Response & Business Continuity

The time to build your incident response capability is before an incident occurs. The organizations that recover fastest from breaches are not the ones with the most expensive tools — they are the ones that practiced, documented, and rehearsed their response before it was needed.

  • Incident response plan development with clearly defined roles, escalation paths, and decision-making authority for every severity level
  • Tabletop exercises and simulation drills that walk your leadership and technical teams through realistic breach scenarios to test decision-making under pressure
  • Forensic investigation capabilities to determine the scope, root cause, and impact of a security event with evidence that supports legal and regulatory requirements
  • Communication protocols covering internal notification chains, customer and partner communications, regulatory reporting obligations, and media response procedures
  • Backup validation and disaster recovery testing to confirm that your backups actually work and your recovery procedures meet their stated objectives
  • Business continuity planning with defined RTO and RPO targets for every critical system, ensuring your organization can continue operating through a disruptive event

Organizations that practice their incident response regularly recover faster, suffer less data loss, and maintain stakeholder trust through transparent and coordinated action.
